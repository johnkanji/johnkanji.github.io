Hello, I am a graphics programmer focusing on animation and simulation. I did my Master's and half a PhD at the Dynamic Graphics Project, University of Toronto where my advisor was Prof. David Levin. My interests are in procedural animation, generative art, emergent systems. I like to work on problems in computer animation, and generally go about that using geometric and data-driven approaches. I'm interested in nearly anything involving motion: character animation, robotics, elastodynamics and more. Before coming to Toronto I did my Bachelors degree at the University of York, United Kingdom where my undergraduate research project was an FPGA implementation of a gyroscopic sensor-fusion algorithm.
